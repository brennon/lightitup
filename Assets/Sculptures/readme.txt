This package contains 3 sculptures and 2 fountains to decorate your scene.
Just drag the prefabs into your scene and place them where you like.

You may also want to have a look at the example scene that shows all objects.
Textures and normal maps are included.

All single parts using about 1600-6000 Polygons.

All textures are baked using ambient occlusion.

The original FBX-files are included in the package.
